# Case Study Example — ACM + IEEE Codes of Ethics

> Reference scenario for practicing ACM/IEEE code application questions.
> Both codes will be provided during the exam — focus on identifying which
> principles are breached or followed, not on memorizing them.

## Scenario

John works as a network engineer for a large company that stores sensitive financial data for thousands of clients. Recently, John discovered a vulnerability in the company's system that could potentially allow unauthorized access to this data. He immediately reports the issue to his supervisor, but the response is disappointing. His supervisor acknowledges the vulnerability but states that fixing it would be too expensive and time-consuming. The supervisor instructs John to document the issue but not take any immediate action.

Two weeks later, John learns that the vulnerability still hasn't been addressed, and now, the company is about to sign a new contract with a major client, guaranteeing the security of their data. John is conflicted about whether he should take further action, as ignoring the issue could lead to serious security breaches, but speaking out might put his job at risk.

## Questions

1. Identify the principles of the **ACM Code of Ethics and Professional Conduct** being breached or followed in this scenario with a brief explanation.
2. Identify the principles of the **IEEE Code of Ethics** being breached or followed in this scenario with a brief explanation.
